Voter Campaign Contribution

About this project

This project aims to track and analyze voter campaign contributions. Our goal is to create an accessible and transparent system that helps people understand the financial aspects of political campaigns.

Weekly Project Process

  • Week 1 - We voted for the project we want to do
  • Week 2 - We have been selected Voter Campaign Contribution Database project and contacted our sponsor by email
  • Week 3 - We meet together on Monday at 4:00 PM and started working on the project, and we decided to meet every Monday at 4:00 PM
  • Week 4 - We meet again on Monday and we keep working on the website and the app
